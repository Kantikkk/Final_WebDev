EXERCISES

Here are exercises for underweight people, who need to get more mass and weight. Our course provides difficulty based on your experience. However if you find it too difficult or too easy, please visit here.

Monday (Strength Training) Tuesday (Cardiovascular Exercise) Wednesday (Rest Day) Thursday (Strength Training) Friday (Cardiovascular Exercise) Saturday (Yoga or Pilates) Sunday (Active Rest Day)
Aim for 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions for each exercise:
  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Push-ups
  • Planks
 Engage in moderate-intensity cardio activities for 30-45 minutes:
  • Swimming
  • Brisk walking
  • Cycling
 Allow your body to recover and rejuvenate. Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions for each exercise:
  • Deadlifts
  • Overhead presses
  • Rows
  • Chest presses
 Alternate between bursts of intense exercise (such as sprinting or jumping jacks) and periods of active rest (such as walking or jogging) for a total of 20-30 minutes. Participate in a yoga or Pilates class to improve flexibility, balance, and core strength. Engage in light activities:
  • Leisurely walking
  • Stretching
  • Gentle yoga
